Introduction

Singing Studios, LLC is a Web App development company with a specialized focus on singing-related Apps. Our feature product is MIDI Karaoke.

MIDI Karaoke

Our feature product is MIDI Karaoke. Practice singing on pitch with this game that
turns any MIDI file into a pitch-scoring game with audio and video recording.

  • Practice singing on pitch with visual feedback
  • Record with vocal effects (reverb, distortion, etc)
  • Easily transpose a song's key to fit your vocal range
  • Two player mode (with second microphone)
  • Optional front-facing camera recording

All Projects

  • MIDI Karaoke

    Practice singing on pitch with this game that turns any MIDI file into a pitch-scoring game with audio and video recording. Includes vocal recording effects, a training mode, and two-player mode (with second mic).

  • Sight-Singer

    Learn to sight-sing with this game that has you sing notes from the major scale as they scroll across the screen. Contains 20 built-in levels that get progressively harder and a custom-level generator.

  • Seven!

    Challenge your math skills with this game. Start with the number seven factorial, and add, subtract, and divide your way through the grid of numbers to find the most efficient path to zero!
